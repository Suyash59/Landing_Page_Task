Our Top Menu

  • Item 1

    • Palak Paneer:

    A classic North Indian dish, Palak Paneer combines creamy spinach with cubes of fresh paneer cheese, delicately spiced with aromatic herbs and served with fluffy naan bread or fragrant basmati rice.

    Price: 350/-


  • Item 2

    • Vegetable Biryani:

    A fragrant and flavorful rice dish cooked with a medley of vegetables, aromatic spices, and saffron-infused basmati rice. The Vegetable Biryani is a vegetarian delight that showcases the rich culinary heritage of India.

    Price: 250/-


  • Item 3

    • Chana Masala:

    This popular Punjabi dish features chickpeas cooked in a tangy tomato-based gravy, infused with a blend of aromatic spices. Served with fluffy naan bread or steamed rice, it's a hearty and satisfying vegetarian option.

    Price: 200/-


  • Item 4

    • Malai Kofta:

    Soft and melt-in-your-mouth vegetable dumplings, made with a mix of mashed potatoes, paneer, and a medley of vegetables, are simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce. This indulgent dish is often paired with buttery naan or fragrant rice.

    Price: 300/-


  • Item 5

    • Aloo Gobi:

    A comforting and flavorful combination of potatoes and cauliflower, stir-fried with a blend of spices, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs. Aloo Gobi is a popular vegetarian dish that pairs well with freshly baked bread or steamed rice.

    Price: 210/-
